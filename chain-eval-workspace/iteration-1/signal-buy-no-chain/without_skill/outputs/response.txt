**On-Chain Analytics Platforms**

1. **Nansen** (nansen.ai) — Labels wallets as "Smart Money" and shows their recent token purchases. You can filter by chain (Ethereum, Solana, etc.) and see aggregated buy activity.

2. **Arkham Intelligence** (arkhamintelligence.com) — Tracks labeled wallets (funds, whales, exchanges) and their on-chain transactions in near real-time.

3. **Dune Analytics** (dune.com) — Community-built dashboards tracking whale/smart money flows. Search for "smart money" dashboards.

4. **Bubblemaps** (bubblemaps.io) — Visualizes token holder clustering and concentration, useful for spotting coordinated accumulation.

**DEX-Focused Tools**

- **DEXScreener** (dexscreener.com) — Shows recent trades, large buys, and trending tokens across chains.
- **DEXTools** (dextools.io) — Real-time trading activity, hot pairs, and large transaction alerts.
- **Birdeye** (birdeye.so) — Solana-focused smart money tracking, trader leaderboards, and token signals.

**Telegram / Alert Bots**

- **Whale Alert** (@whale_alert on Telegram) — Tracks large on-chain transfers.
- **Cielo Finance** (cielo.finance) — Wallet tracking with buy/sell alerts for labeled smart money.

**What to Look For in Buy Signals**

- Large wallet addresses with a profitable trading history suddenly accumulating a token
- Multiple independent smart money wallets buying the same token within a short window
- Tokens with rising volume but still low market cap (early accumulation phase)
- Wallets associated with known funds or high-win-rate traders making new positions

**Limitations to Keep in Mind**

Smart money signals are not guaranteed — even experienced traders make losing trades. Always do your own research (DYOR) and consider:
- The token's fundamentals and use case
- Liquidity and whether you can exit
- Whether the buy pattern might be wash trading or manipulation
- Your own risk tolerance
