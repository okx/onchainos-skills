To find new meme tokens that just launched today in the trenches (pump.fun and similar launchpads), here are your best options:

**Pump.fun (Solana)**
- Go to https://pump.fun and sort by "New" to see tokens launched in the last few hours
- Filter by graduation status, volume, or holder count to spot which ones are getting traction

**DEX Screener**
- https://dexscreener.com — set filter to "New Pairs", chain = Solana (or whichever chain you're watching), time = last 1–6 hours
- Sort by volume or transaction count to identify ones with early activity

**Photon / Bullx / Trojan**
- These Solana trading terminals have built-in new launch scanners with real-time feeds
- Photon (photon-sol.tinyastro.io) and Bullx (bullx.io) show newly created tokens with dev wallet info and bundle detection

**Things to watch for on new meme launches:**
- Dev wallet holding % (high = red flag)
- Bundle activity at launch (snipers)
- Liquidity locked vs. not locked
- Social presence (Twitter/Telegram linked or ghost project)
- Early holder concentration
