I don't have real-time access to on-chain data, so I can't pull a live leaderboard of top Solana traders by PnL for the last 7 days. However, here are the best ways to find this information:

## Options to Find Top Solana Traders (Last 7 Days, by PnL)

### 1. On-Chain Analytics Platforms

**Birdeye (birdeye.so)**
- Go to https://birdeye.so/leaderboard
- Filter by chain: Solana
- Sort by PnL (7D)
- Shows realized + unrealized PnL with wallet addresses

**Cielo Finance (app.cielo.finance)**
- Leaderboard at https://app.cielo.finance/leaderboard
- Supports Solana, sortable by 7-day PnL
- Includes trade count, win rate, and top tokens traded

**GMGN (gmgn.ai)**
- Smart money and whale tracker
- Filter by Solana, time window: 7D
- Ranks by profit in USD

**Dune Analytics (dune.com)**
- Search for community dashboards: "Solana top traders PnL"
- Example query: https://dune.com/discover/content/relevant?q=solana+trader+pnl
- Community-built dashboards updated frequently

### 2. DEX-Specific Leaderboards

**Jupiter (jup.ag)**
- Some DEX aggregators display top traders by volume/PnL

**Raydium / Orca**
- Individual DEX stats pages may show top liquidity providers and traders

### 3. Data APIs (for programmatic access)

If you want to build your own leaderboard:
- **Helius API** (helius.dev) — transaction history and wallet analytics on Solana
- **Solana Beach** (solanabeach.io) — validator and wallet explorer
- **Flipside Crypto** (flipsidecrypto.xyz) — SQL-based on-chain queries

### What "Top Trader by PnL" Typically Means

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Realized PnL | Profit locked in by selling tokens |
| Unrealized PnL | Paper gains on current holdings |
| Win Rate | % of trades that were profitable |
| Volume | Total USD value traded |
| ROI % | Return on capital deployed |

### Caveats

- On-chain PnL calculations vary by platform (cost-basis method, token pricing source, etc.)
- Some "top" wallets may be wash traders or bots
- Leaderboards typically reflect DEX activity only — CEX trades are off-chain and not visible
- A 7-day window can be heavily influenced by short-term market volatility

For the most up-to-date live leaderboard, I'd recommend heading directly to **Birdeye** or **GMGN** — both have excellent Solana trader leaderboards with 7-day filters.
